There were two other things I was always thinking about : the dawn and my appeal. 
Il y avait aussi deux choses à quoi je réfléchissais tout le temps : l'aube et mon pourvoi. 

I would reason with myself, though , and try not to think about them anymore. 
Je me raisonnais cependant et j'essayais de n'y plus penser.

I would stretch out, look at the sky, and force myself to find something interesting about it. 
Je m'étendais, je regardais le ciel, je m'efforçais de m'y intéresser.

It would turn green : that was evening. 
Il devenait vert, c'était le soir. 

I would make another effort to divert my thoughts. 
Je faisais encore un effort pour détourner le cours de mes pensées.

I would listen to my heart beat. 
J'écoutais mon cœur.

I couldn't imagine that this sound which had been with me for so long could ever stop. 
Je ne pouvais imaginer que ce bruit qui m'accompagnait depuis si longtemps put jamais cesser.

I've never really had much of an imagination. 
Je n'ai jamais eu de véritable imagination.

But still I would try to picture the exact moment when the beating of my heart would no longer be going on inside my head. 
J'essayais pourtant de me représenter une certaine seconde où le battement de ce cœur ne se prolongerait plus dans ma tête. 

But it was no use. 
Mais en vain.

The dawn or my appeal would still be there. 
L'aube ou mon pourvoi étaient là.

I would end up telling myself that the most rational thing was not to hold myself back.
Je finissais par me dire que le plus raisonnable était de ne pas me contraindre.

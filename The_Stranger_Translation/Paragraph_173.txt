He was expressing his certainty that my appeal would be granted, but I was carrying the burden of a sin from which I had to free myself. 
Il me disait sa certitude que mon pourvoi serait accepté, mais je portais le poids d'un péché dont il fallait me débarrasser. 

According to him, human justice was nothing and divine justice was everything. 
Selon lui, la justice des hommes n'était rien et la justice de Dieu tout.

I pointed out that it was the former that had condemned me. 
J'ai remarqué que c'était la première qui m'avait condamné.

His response was that it hadn't washed away my sin for all that. 
Il m'a répondu qu'elle n'avait pas, pour autant, lavé mon pêche.

I told him I didn't know what a sin was.
Je lui ai dit que je ne savais pas ce qu'était un péché. 

All they had told me was that I was guilty. 
On m'avait seulement appris que j'étais un coupable.

I was guilty, I was paying for it, and nothing more could be asked of me. 
J'étais coupable, je payais, on ne pouvait rien me demander de plus.

At that point he stood up again, and the thought occurred to me that in such a narrow cell, if he wanted to move around he didn't have many options.
À ce moment, il s'est levé à nouveau et j'ai pensé que dans cette cellule si étroite, s'il voulait remuer, il n'avait pas le choix.

He could either sit down or stand up.
Il fallait s'asseoir ou se lever.
